ABOUT

Arnav Londhe aka

A Very Younger Talent Arnav Londhe Known As AViN By His Passion From Nagpur, Maharashtra! Music & Piano Had Always Been A Passion For Him! AViN Is Known For Progressive House, Big Room & Future Bass Genre's. In Early 2015, Arnav Got Introduced To EDM Music! Where He Saw Hardwell Performing At Tomorrowland. There Itself He Decided To Become A DJ/Music Producer. AViN Got Inspired By Many Artist Like Hardwell, Martin Garrix, KSHMR, Illenium, DubVision,Tom Swoon, Brooks, Manse. MR.VIBE, PL4YFIELDS, SHAAN J, ATREOUS, DHARMIK, PLUSPLAY Played A Very Important Role In His Life.
